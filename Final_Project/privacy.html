Privacy Policy

At Sports Location Search, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you use our website, such as:

  • Your name, email address, and contact information when you sign up for an account.
  • Location data when you search for nearby sports facilities (if you choose to share it).
  • Reviews, ratings, and any other content you contribute to our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Personalize your experience by showing you relevant sports locations based on your preferences.
  • Send updates, notifications, or promotional content (you can opt out anytime).
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel. We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and show you relevant ads. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work properly if you do.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request that we update or delete your information.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you if the changes are significant. We encourage you to review the policy regularly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us.
